**Lessons from the 2026-06-10 flare (rules going forward):**
- No consecutive-day gym sessions until doctor explicitly clears it. Minimum 48h between loaded sessions.
- No couch sleeping at any point of the day, especially not before training. See [[no-couch-sleep-disc-rule]].
- Track NSAID withdrawal window relative to training intensity. The first 1-2 weeks after stopping the NSAID is the most vulnerable period; weight caps may need to drop temporarily during that window.
- Cumulative load is the silent killer. Single sessions within caps look fine on paper but stack across days/conditions.
- The pool coach is a useful diagnostic input source, not just an in-water trainer. Consult her actively on flare debriefs.
- When Dr. Almeida is unreachable: cascade is Hospital da Luz reception (any physiatrist) -> family doctor SNS (urgent slot for medication adjustment) -> Hospital da Luz Atendimento Urgente Permanente walk-in -> Saúde 24 phone triage. ER only for red flags.
